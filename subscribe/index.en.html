Subscribe to ITPUG

This form is for non Italian residents only. Italian residents please subscribe through the Italian website.

Subscribing to ITPUG is easy, and can be done entirely on-line. Although ITPUG's activity is mainly based in Italy and in the Italian language, we do accept foreign members' support.

The annual due fee for 2008 is 10 €.

At the moment, the only way of paying the annual fee is through a bank transfer to our bank account at Banca Toscana. You can subscribe now and pay later. ITPUG treasurer will get in touch with you as soon as possible.

In order to become a member of our non profit organisation, you need to carefully read our statute/charter, which has been officially translated in the English language. For more information:

  • Charter in PDF format, 116 KB

If you accept the terms detailed in the statute and you are willing to become an ITPUG member, please proceed with the on-line subscription to ITPUG.
